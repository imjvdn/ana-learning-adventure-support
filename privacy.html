Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 23, 2026

Ana's Learning Adventure is a children's educational game designed to work without accounts, ads, tracking, analytics, or social features.

Information The App Uses

The app saves gameplay progress on the device so a player can continue their adventure. This progress may include:

  • Completed missions
  • Stars and streaks
  • Answer counts
  • Reward unlocks
  • Avatar customization choices
  • Recent practice summaries

The app does not ask the child for a name, email address, phone number, location, photo, voice recording, contacts, or free-text messages.

iCloud Sync

iCloud Sync is optional and off by default. A grownup can unlock the Grownups area and turn on iCloud Sync to back up and restore progress through the Apple Account already signed in on the device.

When iCloud Sync is enabled, the app stores one private progress snapshot in the signed-in Apple Account's private iCloud database. This snapshot is used only to restore and merge gameplay progress across devices.

What The App Does Not Use

Ana's Learning Adventure does not use:

  • Ads
  • In-app purchases
  • Subscriptions
  • Tracking
  • Third-party analytics SDKs
  • Third-party backend accounts
  • Social sharing
  • Chat or messaging
  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Photo library
  • Location
  • Contacts

Children's Privacy

The app is built for a child-friendly experience. It does not create a separate child account and does not sell or share child data for advertising.
